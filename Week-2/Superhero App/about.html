About This App

This app is a tribute to some of the greatest heroes from the Marvel Universe. Each hero page features an image, a short biography, and detailed power stats.

Whether you're a fan of Iron Man, Thor, Inno-Vee, or Mils— a powerful new hero from South Africa — this app gives you a quick look into their unique strengths and backstories.

Created for fun, learning, and to celebrate powerful characters (both classic and original) who inspire us with their bravery, intelligence, and strength.

Enjoy exploring your favorite heroes!
